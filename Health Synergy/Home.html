What is Telehealth?

Telehealth refers to the use of telecommunications technologies, such as video calls, phone calls, and online platforms, to provide healthcare services remotely. It involves the exchange of medical information from one location to another to facilitate clinical care, consultation, diagnosis, treatment, and education.

Telehealth allows healthcare professionals to connect with patients and other healthcare providers without the need for physical presence.



What Makes a Telehealth Platform?

Video Conferencing:

Enables real-time virtual consultations between healthcare providers and patients.

Secure Communication:

Ensures the confidentiality of patient information through encrypted and secure communication channels.

Electronic Health Records (EHR):

Integrates with electronic health records systems to facilitate seamless access to patient data.

Appointment Scheduling:

Allows patients to schedule and manage virtual appointments easily.

Remote Monitoring Devices:

Supports integration with devices that enable patients to measure and transmit health data for remote monitoring, especially useful for managing chronic conditions.

Prescription Management:

Enables healthcare providers to electronically prescribe medications.

Telemedicine Applications:

Includes features for diagnosis, treatment, and prescription within the virtual platform.

Patient Portals:

Provides a secure online space for patients to access their health records, test results, and other relevant information.

Payment and Billing Integration:

Streamlines the financial aspects of telehealth services, including billing and payment processing.

User-Friendly Interface:

Ensures ease of use for both healthcare providers and patients, promoting a positive user experience.


How we Make Our Website Accessible to Rural Residents?

Optimize for Low Bandwidth:

By designing the website to be lightweight and optimized for low bandwidth, it ensures that users with slower internet connections can still have access to essential features without delays.

Text-Heavy Alternatives:

By providing text-heavy alternatives for graphics and videos, it can help users with low bandwidth , access information more efficiently, as text content generally requires less bandwidth.

Community Outreach:

Community outreach initiatives are organized to raise awareness. Collaborations with local organizations, community leaders, and healthcare providers are established in order to promote the platform and help the users get familiar with the working.

Alternate Communication Channels:

Helpline or email support are made readily available, for users who may face challenges using the website. This enables them to comfortably seek for help whenever required.

Local Language Support:

Availability of helpline and support through local languages enhances accessibility and ensures that users can navigate smoothly and understand the content more effectively
